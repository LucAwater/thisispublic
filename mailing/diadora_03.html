JOIN US TONIGHT IN CELEBRATING THE 25TH ANNIVERSARY OF DIADORA'S 1990 CATALOGUE

Today, a series of events and guerrilla activities will invade the main capitals of Europe and of the US to celebrate the completion of this pack. 7 countries involved, 7 capitals and a wealth of satellite events will animate the cities’ squares and top sneaker stores. On this occasion, the latest addition to the catalogue alongside the in-line version - the V7000 - will be featured in a special “espresso ristretto” limited edition that is extremely faithful to the original. Another 90s replica, the Azzurri collection tracksuit, will be launched in conjunction with the sneakers.

Patta will host the event in Amsterdam at:

Quartier Putain
Oude Kerksplein 4, 1012 GZ Amsterdam
starting from 6 PM till 9 PM






The Diadora V7000 OG & Tracksuit will release on Friday, November 13, 2015, at Patta and Patta.nl.
