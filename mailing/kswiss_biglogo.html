BRINGING BACK THE 90’s WITH THE GSTAAD BIG LOGO
Grounded in court heritage, ready for the streets of 2015

K-Swiss has once again dipped into their vault of heritage designs and silhouettes to bring back one of their most notable styles, the Gstaad Big Logo - updating the style for Fall Winter 15 whilst ensuring their 90’s charm remains. Creating a pack that is grounded in court heritage but ready for the streets of 2015.

The Gstaad was launched in 1986, and was the second silhouette K-SWISS introduced, after only producing the Classic for 20 years. The Gstaad quickly became an overnight success both on and off the court and was brought back earlier this year in its OG colourway.



The Gstaad Big Logo, an update to the OG was originally launched in 1993 and has been brought back in their original colourways, black and white, this September. The five stripe branding has been subtly simplified to create a timeless silhouette, however an oversized logo stitched onto the heal of the shoe in contrasting colour, creates a playful execution to a sneaker that was once the go-to style for professional tennis players from around the world.

The Gstaad Big Logo is available for men at selected retailers around the world and KSWISS.com from today

About K-SWISS
Founded in 1966, K-Swiss is a heritage American tennis brand. During its history, K-Swiss has represented innovation, quality, performance and style. Its signature K-Swiss Classic, the first American leather tennis shoe, made its debut at Wimbledon, and almost 50 years later is still a style staple both on and off the court. Under new leadership, and committed to a fast-paced and significant brand turnaround, K-Swiss is based in Westlake Village, CA. For more information, please visit www.kswiss.com
