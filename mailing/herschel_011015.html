Drawing from vivid coastal dreamscapes captured by the wandering mind, Herschel Supply presents the Accessories Collection for Fall 2015. Seasonally inspired colors, custom prints and a variety of material stories span this comprehensive range of design driven wallets, clutches, pouches, and tech sleeves.

Among the selection of finely crafted leather goods, the artisanal Premium offering is distinguished by its vegetable tanned leather construction and branded brass hardware. The Offset Collection evokes maritime memories with diagonally screen printed color accents, while the immaculate five-piece Napa Collection features an exclusive line of luxurious chrome tanned silhouettes. Oxblood, Navy Pebble and Charcoal Snake also join the selection of classic leather wallet and clutch options.

Durable fabric accessories are enveloped in seasonal hues, such as Windsor Wine, along with a wide array of custom prints, including earth-toned Raindrop Camo, Black Snake, and the playfully patterned Gold Geo.
