5TH DROP CHASE THERMAL

Since last February when the ‘Boom Town Slickers’ adventure began, Slam Jam and Carhartt WIP monthly dropped, always on the 25th, four programs of basic pieces digging into Carhartt’s vault: Putty Canvas, Denim, Reflective and Bandana. Made from a waffle knit 7 gauge, the ‘Chase Thermal’ is the last chapter of this story.

Looking back to the original purpose of thermal fabrics – ensuring warmth – this capsule was specifically constructed from merino wool so as to guarantee a premium performance. A thermal underlayer takes the shape of a Crewneck Sweatshirt and a Hooded Swetshirt, of a long-john Pant and a Short and the set is completed with the iconic Watch Hat.

The 5th drop ‘Chase Thermal’ is available from now on at Slam Jam and Carhartt WIP, both online and in store, the Carhartt WIP store Hartenstraat Amsterdam and at selected stores worldwide such as Colette, Machine-A, End Clothing and many others.
