It’s all about the direction you take, from Paris to LA with love and Magic - Parisian Lifestyle blogger Sophie Trem

The Heritage American tennis brand K-Swiss has teamed up with internationally renowned Parisian Lifestyle blogger, Sophie Trem, from ‘The Other Art of Living’ (TOAOL) to create an exclusive take on the brand’s most iconic shoe, the Classic, launching on 17th September exclusively at colette in Paris (In-store & Online).

Sophie has been a dedicated fan of K-Swiss ever since she was a teenager and went on her first holiday to California, and saw the iconic sneaker all over people’s feet on the street. It was so different to what was happening sartorially in Paris at the time that it grabbed her attention and she has remained fond of the brand and could not wait to put her own spin on this much loved shoe.

Today, Sophie and her blog ‘The Other Art of Living’ are all about choosing your own direction and celebrating the good things in life. Sophie wanted the sneaker to represent her own personal style and journey she has gone on over the last couple of years. Because of this she wanted to feature a compass similar to that she has tattooed on her wrist. Sophie’s compass tattoo stands for her own direction, it’s not a traditional compass pointing you to go east, west, north or south, but the direction that takes you on a journey to the right path, quest to find balance, the right direction that brings you to peace, love and magic. It represents that it is never too late to change your life and that you should listen to yourself, your instincts and go to the places we want to go.



Sophie wanted to bring this very essence and passion to the collaboration. She worked with tattoo artist, Walter Hego, from the much respected Paris based Ink studio, L’Encrerie who created her tattoo, Sophie asked Walter to come up with a compass that represented the journey that K-Swiss has gone on and the journey it is currently on and to remind people that everyone in life has their own destination, and a compass is just a tool and a universal sign of the direction we chose. This very design that was created is etched onto the tongue of the sneaker.



To finish, Sophie placed her blog’s signature logo embroidered onto the heel of the sneaker in fine contrast stitching. Not only is it elegant and timeless, but hopes to serve as a reminder to celebrate the good things and always chose your path.

The sneaker Sophie has transformed is the Classic which features a premium white featherlite leather upper, leather sock liner, mat chrome d-rings, and a memory foam sock liner for comfort.

THE TOAOL X K-SWISS is available for men and women exclusively at colette from September 17th

About K-SWISS
Founded in 1966, K-Swiss is a heritage American tennis brand. During its history, K-Swiss has represented innovation, quality, performance and style. Its signature K-Swiss Classic, the first American leather tennis shoe, made its debut at Wimbledon, and almost 50 years later is still a style staple both on and off the court. Under new leadership, and committed to a fast-paced and significant brand turnaround, K-Swiss is based in Westlake Village, CA. For more information, please visit www.kswiss.com
