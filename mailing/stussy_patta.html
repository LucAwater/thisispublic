In celebration of Patta’s 10 year anniversary, the Amsterdam-based store and brand have worked together with the legendary streetwear brand Stüssy to produce a capsule apparel collection.
The collection consists of various co-branded pieces, including a fishtail parka, button up shirt, pullover tracksuit, and various graphic t-shirts, longsleeves, crewnecks and hoodies. In addition, the collection is complemented with headwear and various accessories.

The full collection is set to launch January 23 at Patta Amsterdam and the Stüssy New York Chapter Store.

The Stüssy x Patta Capsule Collection is available for press at our showroom. You’re welcome to have a closer look at the collection.
We also have the Stüssy men/women SS14 collection available at our showroom.
