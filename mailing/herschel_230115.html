Drawing from the concentration of mid-century architecture in the city of Palm Springs, Herschel Supply presents the Desert Modernism inspired Spring 2015 Classics Collection. The diversity of the landscape in this region has led to an extensive offering of new and seasonally refreshed bag, luggage and accessory silhouettes.

Engaging seasonal prints include the desert stargazing inspired Polka Dot and geometric Sequence, which pulls from shapes that compliment modernist dwellings. The Kingston print reveals a palm oasis, and Orchard offers a subtle monochromatic dose of citrus fruit. Caramel, Foliage and sky blue Chambray Crosshatch are new additions to the range of desert tones, joining Flamingo, Neon and Washed Mango as vibrant hues that celebrate the season.
