25 years have passed since Edwin Faeh founded “Work in Progress” in the early 90s, becoming the exclusive distributor of Carhartt in Europe. Before that time, Carhartt workwear products had been hard to source outside of the US.

To celebrate the first 25 years, some of the most iconic workwear styles from the Carhartt archives, along with a selection of classic pieces from Carhartt WIP, have been re-designed and re-interpreted, shaking and twisting the original fabrics, colours and trims.

All 25 products come complete with silver coloured hardware and labels (to symbolise a traditional 25th anniversary), together with a heart shaped button as tribute to the original Carhartt logo.



The CARHARTT WIP 25 YEARS PROGRESS collection will drop tomorrow on the 25th of September at the Carhartt WIP Amsterdam store (Hartenstraat 18) and selected retailers worldwide.
