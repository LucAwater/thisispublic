Global accessories brand Herschel Supply presents the Nylon Collection for Fall 2015. Combining everyday functionality with an elevated aesthetic, a line of new and classic silhouettes are constructed with a reinforced lightweight nylon fabric and finished with premium details.

Available in timeless Black or Navy tones, each piece is defined by clean design. The extensive backpack selection includes Herschel Supply classics like the Heritage, Lawson, and Little America, joined by the elegant Reid and the Dawson — a new women’s silhouette for Fall. Distinctive renditions of the quintessential Novel weekender and snap-down Strand complete the range of duffle bag options.

The spacious and everyday Alexander tote delivers convenient storage and a refined aesthetic. Compact carrying options such as the Network pouches and Chapter travel kit offer versatile options for organizing your essentials.



Smooth leather straps, zipper pulls and snap mounts complement this nylon offering, while polished metal hardware and a custom black and white rendition of Herschel Supply’s signature striped liner accents each piece.

The Herschel Supply Nylon Collection for Fall 2015 is available at Baskets.
