DIADORA CELEBRATES “THE LAST GREAT DECADE” WITH A CAPSULE FROM THE 1990 CATALOGUE

The tv-shows, the indipendent cinemas, the long summer nights. Grunge, pop, heavy metal. The Walkman, the first CDs, the boombox. The icons that made the 90s the de- cade of big nostalgia mixed with a technological push towards the future, serve as the backdrop to the new collection of Diadora Sportswear FW15. This inspiration echoes through thanks to sporty citations, in a play of juxtapositions which make this proposal a tribute to the most contradictory decade of the twentieth century. From a transposed page of the 1990 running catalog comes a “retro ‘running” capsule encompassing the entire range of running equipment, with a version for training, a ra- cing staple and a style for all those runners in need of greater cushioning. Each of these styles is made in Italy.



On 13 November, a series of events and guerrilla activities will invade the main capitals of Europe and of the US to celebrate the completion of this pack. 7 countries involved, 7 capitals and a wealth of satellite events will animate the cities’ squares and top sneaker stores. On this occasion, the latest addition to the catalogue alongside the in-line version - the V7000 - will be featured in a special “espresso ristretto” limited edition that is extremely faithful to the original. Another 90s replica, the Azzurri collection tracksuit, will be launched in conjunction with the sneakers.

Patta will host the event on the 13th of November in Amsterdam at:
