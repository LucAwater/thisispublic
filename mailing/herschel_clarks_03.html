This Fall, Herschel Supply and Clarks Originals have come together for the first time to produce an adventurous spin on one of Clarks’ classic silhouettes.

Renowned for their iconic designs, Clarks Originals represents a style that has stood the test of time, respected by generations past and present. Re-imagining the popular Desert Boot silhouette, the Herschel Supply and Clarks Originals partnership gives rise to a unique boot, tailor made to take the great outdoors or city in stride.

The simplicity of the Desert Boot provided the ideal foundation for combining textures, making it a natural fit for an interesting mixed material story. Rendered in premium leather and selvage denim, the navy blue boot is fully lined with Herschel Supply’s signature red and white striped liner, sitting atop the Clarks Originals eponymous crepe sole. This new incarnation upholds the practicality and style of the Clarks Originals brand, while pushing the boundaries with Herschel Supply’s design-driven characteristics. In essence, the partnership represents the coming together of two like minds.

The Herschel Supply and Clarks Originals Desert Boot in navy blue is available at selected retailers.
