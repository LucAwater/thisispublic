4TH DROP BANDANA

The ‘Boom Town Slickers’ journey continues and now it’s the turn of the 4th drop: the Bandana program.

Keeping on exploring Carhartt WIP’s archive, Slam Jam picked up some of the Spring/Summer essentials made from 100% cotton and turned them into contemporary pieces thanks to the bandana pattern.

Finding inspiration in the original Carhartt bandana design, the capsule takes note of the playful graphics and interpret them for the warm season to come. The bandana size has been exploded to take the form of a giant square Beach Towel whilst a patchwork has been made from the original size and turned into a Float Swim Trunk, a Simple tote, a reversible Bucket hat and a Kansas shirt.

The 4th drop ‘Bandana’ will be available from May 25th at Slam Jam and Carhartt WIP, both online and in store, the Carhartt WIP store Hartenstraat Amsterdam and at selected stores worldwide such as Colette, Machine-A, End Clothing and many others.
