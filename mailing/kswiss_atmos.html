K-SWISS PARTNERS WITH SNEAKER LABEL ATMOS ON LIMITED EDITION SI-18 INTERNATIONAL SNEAKER

Heritage American tennis brand K-Swiss and Japanese sneaker boutique atmos debut a collaborative update to K-Swiss’ iconic Si-18 International silhouette. The limited edition sneaker, dubbed the Si-18 International Sakura Blossom, marries K-Swiss’ roots in court sports with design cues from Japan’s national flower, the sakura (cherry blossom). Retailing for $125.00, the K-Swiss x atmos Si-18 International Sakura Blossom is available now in limited quantities at the Atmos Tokyo store and online at www.atmos-tokyo.com.

“The Si-18 International has always been one of our biggest icons and we really wanted to be able to bring this to the next level with atmos,” said Jon Tang, lead designer for K-Swiss. “All the stars aligned when we began to piece our inspirations together.”

Drawing direct inspiration from a tennis court surrounded by full bloom sakura tress, the K-Swiss x Atmos Si-18 International Sakura has a vibrant pink upper to symbolize the color of the Japanese blossoms. The upper is built with a tennis ball-inspired felt that lends to a breathable, lightweight and flexible design. Additional design details include a sakura blossom print lining and K-Swiss’ iconic five-stripe branding created with reflective materials. A matching pink outsole, lacing and eyelet accents offer a tonal aesthetic bringing a statement finish to any look.



The K-Swiss x Atmos Si-18 International Sakura Blossom is the first drop in a special collaboration series that celebrates tennis courts around the world. Additional Si-18 International collaborations will be released in limited quantities throughout the year, including shoes from Scotland (hanon Shop), Sweden (Sneakernstuff) and the United States (Packer Shoes).

About K-SWISS
Founded in 1966, K-Swiss is a heritage American tennis brand. During its history, K-Swiss has represented innovation, quality, performance and style. Its signature K-Swiss Classic, the first leather tennis shoe, made its debut at Wimbledon, and almost 50 years later is still a style staple both on and off the court. Under new leadership, and committed to a fast-paced and significant brand turnaround, K-Swiss is based in Westlake Village, CA. For more information, please visit www.kswiss.com

About Atmos
With a sneaker empire that spans from Japan to New York City, Atmos first opened its doors in Harajuku in 2000. What sets Atmos apart from your average sneaker boutique is not only the most sought after limited editions they have been able to offer, but also the amount of coveted collaborations they’ve done with the best brands in the industry season after season. Five years into being the premier sneaker destination in Japan, Atmos set their sights on the U.S. and opened its doors on the famed 125th Street in Harlem, New York. For more information, please visit www.atmos-tokyo.com.
