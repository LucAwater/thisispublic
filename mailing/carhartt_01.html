3RD DROP REFLECTIVE

With the first two drops, the ‘Boom Town Slickers’ collection introduced the Putty Canvas and the Denim programs. Following the exploration of the Carhartt WIP roots, for the third drop Slam Jam and Carhartt WIP chose to work on basic workwear items using a special material.

Carhartt WIP use reflective panels and taping across many of their high-visibility and safety-wear pieces. Treating this as a stylistic influence, the Reflective program offers a similar taping and printing in two colours - Black and White, with tonal trims - across S/S Pocket T-Shirt, Hooded Chase Jacket, Mosley Jacket, Vest, Adams Duffle bag.

The 3rd drop ‘Reflective’ will be available from April 25th at Slam Jam and Carhartt WIP, both online and in store, the Carhartt WIP store Hartenstraat Amsterdam and at selected stores worldwide such as Colette, Machine-A, End Clothing and many others.
