Global accessories brand Herschel Supply proudly presents the Herschel Hounds Collection for Fall 2015. Paying homage to the town of Herschel’s coveted varsity team, this versatile backpack, duffle, accessory and headwear offering embodies the spirit of competition and the traditional value of sportsmanship.

Originally sketched on a napkin in the Herschel Cafe, the long-standing Hounds logo is a cherished symbol that adorns each piece in this collection. Available in Home (navy and red) or Away (black and grey) colors, the essence of the Hounds is captured with athletic heritage inspired details. Soft heather fleece lines each silhouette, while striped rubber and nylon webbing, cotton accents and pebbled leather elevate this premium selection.



Designed to inspire performance both on and off the playing surface, the Heritage and Little America backpacks join the Anchor Sleeve for iPad and MacBook, which conveys the casual comfort of vintage sporting goods. Both the Sutton Mid duffle and Network Extra Large pouch feature heather fleece panels detailed with Herschel Hounds screen printed lettering.

In partnership with sportswear brand Starter®, fine quality headwear silhouettes complete this offering. The Team and Club cotton snapback caps are finished with custom detailing that is synonymous with both the Hounds and Herschel Supply.

The Herschel Hounds Collection for Fall 2015 is available at ROYAL sneakers.
