Carhartt WIP (Work in Progress) was born 25 years ago from workwear specialists Carhartt, and year over year has continued to define workwear apparel for new generations through innovative and authentic collections. The clothing has been embraced by a range of underground subcultures around the world while remaining true to the brand’s founding values of quality, comfort and durability.

Exploring a shared history in streetwear, Stüssy and Carhartt WIP collaborate on iconic workwear staples with modern updates. The collection includes a chore coat, vest, lux zip hood, ruck single knee pant, bucket hat and beanie, produced by Carhartt WIP with Stüssy signature graphics.



The Stüssy Carhartt WIP Collection will be available at the Carhartt WIP Amsterdam store (Hartenstraat 18, Amsterdam), Stüssy Chapter Stores, Stussy.com and Carhartt WIP retailers on Friday, November 20th.

The collection editorial was shot in New York by Ari Marcopoulos with styling by Tom Guinness.
