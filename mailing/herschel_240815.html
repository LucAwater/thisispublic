Uniquely crafted to compliment a female silhouette, global accessories brand Herschel Supply proudly introduces the Women’s Collection for Fall 2015. Both flattering and functional, this inaugural offering of backpack, tote, hip purse and clutch silhouettes are elevated by lightweight fabrics and elegant finishings.

Amongst the range of backpacks, the contemporary Ware and leather drawcord detailed Hanson offer cinch closures, while the Dawson redefines an iconic mountaineering style. Each piece features slender shoulder straps and supplementary side-access zippered sleeves.

The extended handles of the Richmond tote make it perfect for shouldering everyday essentials, and the Carter clutches fold over with the use of unique hidden magnetic closures. Designed to be worn around the waist or as a crossbody, the versatile Scarlett and compact Brooke hip purses complete this comprehensive feminine selection.



Inspired by bountiful vineyards, rich and luscious Windsor Wine is among the seasonal hues that adorn the line, joined by the off-white Natural, Grey and Navy Crosshatch. Brass hardware and pebbled leather details feature prominently across the collection, along with Herschel Supply’s signature striped liner and classic woven label.

The Herschel Supply Women’s Collection for Fall 2015 is available at Funkie House, Nr. 9, The Little Green Bag, Shop Noir and Tally-ho.
