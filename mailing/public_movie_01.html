The biannual tradeshows during Berlin Fashion Week are a must visit for PUBLIC_.
Brands offer their new collections, we meet (new) friends, and most of all we get inspired.

During our latest visit to Berlin, we managed to make two videos.

In the first one, we asked Daily Paper’s co-founders Jefferson Osei and Abderrahmane Trabsini everything about Daily Paper’s newest Spring 2016 Collection. Daily Paper offers us a sneak preview of the collection, describing their heritage.

The video was shot in Berlin at the SEEK tradeshow and at the Daily Paper pop-up store.



In the second one, we asked Herschel Supply’s Global Marketing Director Mikey Scott everything about the Herschel Supply Spring 2016 Collection.He also shows a sneak preview of the Studio Collection.

This video was shot in Berlin at the SEEK tradeshow.
