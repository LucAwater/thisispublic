Crafted from premium fine grain leather with a chrome tanned sheen, global accessories brand Herschel Supply introduces the luxurious Napa Collection for Fall 2015. Exclusive wallet and clutch styles comprise this immaculate offering, uniting soft black Napa leather with design driven details.

Five brand new silhouettes join the collection, with compact options including the slender Slip card holder and the timeless Merritt bi-fold. Featuring sleek and stylish storage options, the brass zippered Lamont offers an extended knotted pull, while the Tokyo and Olympic wallets feature a custom snap closure.



Accentuating the smooth and flawless Napa leather exterior are custom Herschel Supply details. Each piece is lined with a tonal polka dot nylon, along with tasteful exterior accents that include signature red and white stitching and the distinguished black and gold toned ID logo hardware.

The Herschel Supply Napa Collection for Fall 2015 is available at Baskets.
