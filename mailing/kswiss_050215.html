K-Swiss introduced the first all leather tennis shoe to players in 1966, changing the game forever. In 1986, 20 years after the release of the Original Classic and adhering to the same spirit of innovation, K-Swiss introduced a new tennis silhouette called the Gstaad.

The re-release of this legendary tennis shoe is the first in a series of special colorway releases rolling out through the rest of 2015. This re-release has been built with the exact craftsmanship of the debut model in 1986 and is made with premium grade leather. The Gstaad for women are available at premium fashion/sneaker retailers from now on.
