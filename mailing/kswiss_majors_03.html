To celebrate a series of the most prestigious tennis tournaments in the world K-Swiss announces the MAJORS #3 pack, featuring the first ever women’s Si-18 International and matching Gstaad and color updates of the Si-18 International and Gstaad for men.

Heritage American tennis brand K-Swiss has just announced the drop of their latest pack Majors #3 at selected sneaker boutiques around the world and KSWISS.com at 13th August. The pack includes the brands first ever Women’s Si-18 International and color updates of the Si-18 International and Gstaad for men. It is part of an ongoing series of ‘Majors’ launches that will continue throughout the year, to help celebrate the most coveted tennis tournaments around the world.

With the US open just weeks away, the Majors #3 pack takes two heritage tennis performance styles and updates them into sneakers that can win in the style stakes off-court. The pack, made up of two silhouettes, the Si-18 International and Gstaad are available for both men and women.

The women’s colorway take influence from the first ever Si-18 International that launched in 1989, however our designers have softened up the pop details of red with Rose of Sharon and the deep blue with a lighter softer hue, creating a more feminine approach to an iconic silhouette.



The men’s color updates are totally fresh for 2015 and are influenced by the height of summer – the premium white leather upper has been modernized with color-pops of stand-out blue, influenced by the US courts and a limepunch green that was inspired by the color of the tennis balls, creating a pack of sneakers that nods to the brands US tennis DNA, whilst remaining fresh for the streets of 2015.



All four sneakers within the Majors #3 pack are made to the same high specification and exact craftsmanship as the original models and is the latest addition to the series of special colourway releases and collaborations rolling out through 2015.

THE K-SWISS MAJORS #3 PACK IS AVAILABLE AT SELECTED RETAILERS AND AT WWW.KSWISS.COM FROM THE 13TH AUGUST

About K-SWISS
Founded in 1966, K-Swiss is a heritage American tennis brand. During its history, K-Swiss has represented innovation, quality, performance and style. Its signature K-Swiss Classic, the first American leather tennis shoe, made its debut at Wimbledon, and almost 50 years later is still a style staple both on and off the court. Under new leadership, and committed to a fast-paced and significant brand turnaround, K-Swiss is based in Westlake Village, CA. For more information, please visit www.kswiss.com
