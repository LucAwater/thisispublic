Australian label P.A.M. and Carhartt WIP join forces again to offer a women’s capsule collection that will land in selected Carhartt WIP Store and Carhartt WIP retailers the 26th of November. One inspired by industry that echoes in workwear styling and the eternal forces of nature and the natural world. Ashio Dozen is a collection and a place. It is a town where ghosts, destruction, nature and beauty all meet, it’s not a clash rather they sit in silent harmony.

Post apocalyptic feelings ooze from every broken window and collapsed factory roof. Once an industrious copper mine but now 100 years after environmental poisoning it’s abandoned and has become a ghost town. The natural world has reclaimed the territory, vines covering buildings, rain rots the wood so in the end what we see is a beautiful world in a slow transition. With only small hints that humans were once there.



P.A.M. celebrates the natural world with mushroom prints and ops for Carhartt WIP outdoors utilitarian styling.

Have a look at the products and at the lookbook shot on location at Ashio Dozen by Shauna T (P.A.M.) at carhartt-wip.com

The P.A.M. X Carhartt WIP Collection will be available at at selected Carhartt WIP Stores, selected retailers and online at carhartt-wip.com on Thursday, November 26th.
