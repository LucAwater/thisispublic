Global accessories brand Herschel Supply presents the Spring 2015 Kids Collection. Thoughtfully designed to be functional and fun, a sized down offering of popular silhouettes are featured in a wide range of playful seasonal colors and custom prints.

Perfect for the school cafeteria, the wipeable and fully drainable Canteen lunch bag is a fresh new piece for Spring. Both stylish and durable enough for the playground, the Settlement, Heritage and Survey Kids backpacks – sized for ages six and under – join a slightly larger Settlement Youth backpack intended for children seven and up.

The Kids Collection features exposed plastic zippers, along with full grain leather and rubber detailing. Inside lies Herschel Supply’s telltale signature striped fabric liner and a customizable woven name label.

The Spring 2015 Kids Collection is available in the Netherlands at Broer & zus, Grom, Bon bon on the block, Big & belg, Springstof, Orange mayonaise, Mini market and The little green bag.
