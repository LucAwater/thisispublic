CELEBRATING THE #HSDECADE


Continuing to work with some of the world‘s most iconic streetwear brands, Highsnobiety teamed up with Carhartt WIP on a monochromatic 10th anniversary collection. This capsule is characterized by the monochromatic colors that define the Highsnobiety aesthetic, alongside the cities where the publication‘s teams are based: New York and Berlin.

Regarding this celebratory project, the Highsnobiety editorial team related the following: “As one of just a handful of brands that have continued to not only remain relevant but push the game forward, our collaboration with Carhartt WIP gives back to the culture that helped birth it through a number of timeless staples.”



Dropping December 4, 2015 exclusively at SOTO Berlin and Carhartt WIP‘s Paris Le Marais, London Earlham Street, Berlin Mitte, New York, Milan Ticinese, Tokyo and Hong Kong Silvercord stores.

Beginning December 4, 14:00 CET the following online outlets will also stock the collection: Carhartt WIP, SOTO Berlin and Highsnobiety‘s e-store.
