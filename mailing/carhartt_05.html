ACOUSTIC SESSION INVITE

Carhartt WIP presents the Oddisee & Good Compny Euro Tour this fall. To warm up Amsterdam for his live gig at Paradiso on Sept 16th, we are inviting you to an intimate acoustic session at the Carhartt WIP store!

DATE: Monday the 14th of September
TIME: 19.00 hrs
LOCATION: CARHARTT WIP STORE, Hartenstraat 18, Amsterdam

Oddisee, the man from Washington, D.C. who is currently based in Brooklyn, NYC travels across Europe to present The Good Fight. For himself, his tenth album in seven years is "about living fully as a musician without succumbing to the traps of hedonism, avarice, and materialism."

His lyrics are direct and his tunes are full of musicality to unite Jazz sounds of organs, brass horns or cymbals with heavy grooving rhythms that go right to your core. On stage he performs his eclectic soulful boom-bap full of meaningful messages with his band Good Compny to showcase his tunes with a deep organic live flavor.
